Who We Are

CivoraTech is a dynamic team formed under the ICET ICD/ICP Developer Training Program. We focus on transforming the postal services sector, enhancing efficiency, accessibility, and digital integration through intelligent technology.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between citizens and governance using cutting-edge solutions that promote transparency, efficiency, and accessibility in public services.
